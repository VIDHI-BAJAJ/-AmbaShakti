LPG CYLINDER

The group started its commercial production of LPG Cylinders from September,2001 by the name and style of S. M. Cylinders. It Works at B-2, Site-IV, UPSIDC, Greater Noida, Distt. : Gautam Budh Nagar, U.P. for Public Sector Undertakings( PSUs ) like Indian Oil Corporation Ltd., Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Ltd. and Bharat Petroleum Corporation Ltd. The installed capacity of the plant is 12,00,000 Cylinders per annum. The Company also takes orders for repairing of old LPG Cylinders for above mentioned Public Sector Undertakings and has already been granted Bureau of Indian Standards Certification Marks for manufacture and repair of LPG Cylinders by producing quality products. The Company has always achieved its targets.

REAL ESTATE

In continuance of our endeavors towards the infrastructural development of our country, AMBA group initiated its contribution by diversifying into land and real estate projects. Through A D Real build Ltd, an Amba group company, the group invests in lands in R zone in Delhi NCR. The project envisions creation of modern multistory residential zones along with commercial spaces.
