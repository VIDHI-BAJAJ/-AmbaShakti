REBARS:

SYMBOL OF QUALITY AND TRUST

With a huge support and commendation from millions of satisfied customers and clients across the nation, Amba Shakti TMT bar has become synonym of trust and confidence with its premier unmatched quality steel products. Amba Shakti Group has been constantly fulfilling various social responsibilities to do its bit towards the growth of the nation and a greener planet through the best possible means. The direct rolling technology is eco-friendly as it uses the latent heat of the metal and avoid carbon emissions which is not possible for other plants.

Rebars

Chemistry : Amba Shakti TMT bars are made with a controlled chemistry of carbon, sulphur, phosphorous and other alloying elements. Carbon equivalent is maintained in a lower range to facilitate good weldability.

Rolling:The billets are rolled in most advanced mills with all PLC controlled drives for maintaining very narrow range of temperatures minimum rolling time and other rolling parameters which are critical in making the rebar super ductile.

Thermo Mechanical Treatment :The Tempcore TMT online quenching process is adopted after rolling with automated control of water pressure, nozzle angle and the rate of water flow.
